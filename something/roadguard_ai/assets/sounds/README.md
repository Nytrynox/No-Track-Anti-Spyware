# Sound Assets

Place your alert sounds here:

- `collision_warning.mp3` - Critical collision alert
- `lane_departure.mp3` - Lane departure warning
- `speed_alert.mp3` - Speed limit exceeded

## Recommended Specifications

- Format: MP3 or WAV
- Duration: 0.5 - 1.5 seconds
- Sample rate: 44100 Hz
- Clear, attention-grabbing sounds
- Not too harsh or startling
